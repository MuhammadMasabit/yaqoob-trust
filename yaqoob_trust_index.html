Who We Are

A Society Built on
Compassion.

Yaqoob Trust — Ray of Hope — is a volunteer-driven social welfare society working tirelessly to serve humanity. We believe every person deserves dignity, health, and support.

From emergency blood arrangements to helping needy families with food and essentials, our dedicated team works across 6 major cities of Pakistan with love and sincerity.

Our Mission
Spreading Hope, One Life at a Time

We work as one family to ensure no one is left behind in times of need. Every act of kindness — big or small — matters deeply.

Blood Donation

Your Blood Can
Save a Life Today.

We arrange emergency blood donations 24/7. Contact us and we will connect you with the right donor immediately — completely free of cost.

🩸
Emergency Blood

We arrange blood on urgent basis for patients in hospitals. No patient is ever turned away from our help.

🏥
Hospital Support

We coordinate with hospitals across all 6 cities to ensure blood is available when it is needed the most.

📋
Donor Registration

Register as a blood donor with us. You will be contacted when your blood group is urgently needed.

🤝
Completely Free

All blood donation arrangements are 100% free of cost. We only need your willingness to save a life.

⚡
24/7 Available

Our team is available around the clock every single day. Emergencies do not wait and neither do we.

💪
All Blood Groups

A+, A−, B+, B−, O+, O−, AB+, AB− — we have donors for all blood groups in our network.
